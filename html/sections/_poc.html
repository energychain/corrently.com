Process flow (PoC)

(note: Corrently invest token has 2 decimals!)

Our Proof of Concept was done mainly in the previously mentioned domain blockchain. However, we ported all the money, ownership and asset related contracts to Ethereum Main Blockchain. The following transaction flow provides public proof of our concept and business model.

STROMDAO confirms profit share agreement of a provider
  1. STROMDAO does assesment and valuation (=10€ per month)
  2. Issue 2000 new tokens
  3. transfer 2000 tokens to provider
Customer buys CORI out of loyalty programme
  1. Customer exchanges 200 Corrently
  2. Transfer of Corrently from provider to customer
  3. STROMDAO ensures electricity bill of customer is reduced by 0,10€ each month
Operator transfers Ether as Dividend - Shareholder withdraws
  1. Operator transfers 0.001 Ether to profit sharing smart contract
  2. Shareholder requests withdraw and receives ether.
  3. STROMDAO ensures electricity bill of customer is reduced by 0,10€ each month
