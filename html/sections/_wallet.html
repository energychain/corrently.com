Notes/Help

As CORI is based on public Ethereum BlockChain, you might use any tool to access, trade or manage our token. We always include MetaMask, MyEtherWallet, and EtherScan in our tests. In order to work with a SmartContract in Ethereums main blockchain, published address ( 0x725b190bc077ffde17cf549aa8ba25e298550b18 ) and an ABI definition is required.

Intend of this page is not to replace high-level tools. Instead, it should provide you with a quick access and validation capabilities.
