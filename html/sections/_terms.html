Redemption and profit sharing policy

Shareholders within a valid STROMDAO tariff receive shared profit automatically (aprox. 2000 Tokens required for a monthly reduction of 1€, based on actually generation in payment cycle).

All token holders have the freedom to share, trade, sell and buy Corrently Invest Tokens (CORI) at any time. As STROMDAO we try to ensure listing on all major decentralized world-wide exchanges (at the moment: EtherDelta, Token.Store and Instex).

  • Customers could reduce their bills further by buying additional tokens from the market.
  • Operators/physical asset owners (party that shares profit) could buy back obligations they sold.

Clearing is done monthly within the consens of a single block in Ethereum public blockchain (aka: Homestead). STROMDAO is entitled by its customers (electricity consumers) to collect debits from property owners and deduct customer electricity bills.
