Airdrop

Corrently is an entirely new product in the electricity market. Our approach is to accelerate the expansion of renewable energy generation by giving each electricity customer the possibility to do micro-investments. Instead depending on governmental funds each owner of renewable energy generation facilities can offer their wind-mill, PV-panel or water turbine to be tokenized by STROMDAO.

Corrently Invest Token

Primary purpose of Corrently Invest Token (CORI) is a profit distribution coming out of those micro investments. CORI shareholders get their energy bills deducted by the shared profit automatically. Shareholders without an electricity tariff receive dividends by requesting a withdraw via a smart-comtract.

We believe in transparency and think it should start with an public visible accounting. Most crypto/blockchain startups we have seen, lack a possibility to pay dividends. Therefore Thorsten published a Medium article that describes how we do profit distribution in Ethereum's smart contracts.

  1. Get Informed about Corrently
  2. Figure out the address of the profit sharing contract we used in our proof of concept.
  3. Transfer a tiny amount of ether to this contract (smaller 0.001 should be fine - just be sure you provide enough gas for transfer).
  4. You could transfer 0 ETH - however you will not be able to see how provid sharing works!
  5. Enter transaction hash into the form below.
  6. Receive your 0.01 CORI Token in return.
  7. Return to this page (or our wallet) to see how your 0.01 CORI entitles for dividend.
  8. At some later time - sell your CORIs on any decentralized exchange supporting ERC20 or use withdraw function to get dividend payed in ETH.
