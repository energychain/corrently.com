Corrently - Brazil - Airdrop

Corrently follows Brazil's legislation on remote consumption of distributed energy, in which consumers can rent a quota from a solar plant or windmill that is not allocated on their land.

Corrently is an entirely new product in the electricity market. Our approach is to accelerate the expansion of renewable energy generation by giving each electricity customer the possibility to do micro-investments. Instead depending on governmental funds each owner of renewable energy generation facilities can offer their wind-mill, PV-panel or water turbine to be tokenized by STROMDAO Listed exchanges available for Brazil market: TokenStore, EtherDelta, Instex.
